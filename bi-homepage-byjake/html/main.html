ABOUT US
• Our mission is to set the standard of the art market to analyze and to trade

• We believe that an investment aspect of the art market can be provided to a wider audience by data aggregation, transparency, and analytics, similar to services by EDGAR and Yahoo!Finance, before providing any fraction ownership or investment related opportunities or services

• Usage of blockchain can validate and actively support market-to-market valuation under Blue Index’s platform ecosystem

• We do not expect owners of works to register information on ownership, provenance, exhibition, price of artworks on our registry, but we do expect market experts and specialists to participate and to cross check the accuracy of the information on the platform through our incentivized economy (detail information is undisclosed)

• Over the years, we developed internal software solutions to support data transparency and analytics

SEOUL AUCTION BLUE

Seoul Auction Blue is a subsidiary of the largest auction House in Korea, Seoul Auction, focusing on IT and finance of auction services. Seoul Auction Blue works closely with major global auction houses, such as Christie’s, Sotheby’s, and Phillips.
