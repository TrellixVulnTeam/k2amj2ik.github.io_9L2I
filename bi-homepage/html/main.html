ABOUT US
Blue Index aims to lead the development of the art industry and culture by providing software tools, such as analytic platform, data services, and news to category related dealers, auctioneers, collectors, and financial companies to analyze and to monitor the market and its trends.
PARTNERS

Korea Art Management Service

Korea Art Management Service, an affiliate of Korea’s Ministry of Culture, Sports, and Tourism, offers diverse research, consulting and educational programs for those in the field to boost the industrial competitiveness of the Korean arts.

HANWHA SYSTEMS

Hanwha Systems is a subsidiary of one of Korea’s largest conglomerate, Hanwha Group. As one of the leading corporations in Korea to focus on implementation of blockchain technology, Hanwha System acts as a strategic investor and collaborator to Blue Index.

SEOUL AUCTION BLUE

Seoul Auction Blue is a subsidiary of the largest auction House in Korea, Seoul Auction, focusing on IT and finance of auction services. Seoul Auction Blue works closely with major global auction houses, such as Christie’s, Sotheby’s, and Phillips.
